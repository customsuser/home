There are questions about how much of a red-card offense Ghafurov committed on Odisha’s Moroccan midfielder Ahmed Zahu. But Gafurov has been banned for two matches by the AFC Disciplinary Committee for this crime. Also, the Uzbek midfielder will have to pay a fine of 1000 dollars or 100,000 taka.  

After the 10-man loss to Odisha, Kings coach Oscar Brujon was unreservedly critical of the referee at a press conference. Bruzon was at risk of a ban or fine after so openly criticizing the referee in that December 11 match. However, the Disciplinary and Ethics Committee of the Asian Football Confederation (AFC) did not take any action against him. However, they considered the crime of Uzbek footballer Asor Gafurov serious.  

The decision of the AFC Disciplinary and Ethics Committee meeting on January 15 was announced on the organization’s website.
