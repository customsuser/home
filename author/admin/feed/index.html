In November last year, there were rumors that Lionel Scaloni will soon leave the responsibility of the Argentina national team. In December, ESPN reported that this coach will not be seen in Argentina’s dugout after the Copa America.This World Cup winning coach has dismissed these rumours.

Scaloni commented, ‘I’ve always said it’s time to stop and think. It wasn’t goodbye. There were only reflections of that moment. Now we must continue. ‘
